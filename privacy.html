Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Evolution Quantum Capital (EQC) ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our website at eqc.investments and our social media presence, including our TikTok integration through the TikTok Content Posting API.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data: Anonymous data about how visitors interact with our website, collected through tools such as Google Analytics (page views, session duration, referral sources).
  • TikTok account data: When we connect our TikTok account to publish content via the TikTok Content Posting API, we store an access token and account identifier (open_id) for our own EQC TikTok account only. We do not collect data from other TikTok users.
  • Contact information: If you contact us directly (e.g. via social media), we may retain your username or message for the purpose of responding to you.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Publish financial analysis and educational content to our TikTok, X (Twitter), and Instagram accounts via their respective APIs.
  • Monitor and improve the performance of our website.
  • Respond to user enquiries or community messages.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

3. TikTok API Integration

EQC uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish video and photo content to our official TikTok account (@evolutionqc) on our own behalf. This integration:

  • Only accesses and posts to our own EQC TikTok account.
  • Does not collect, store, or process data from any other TikTok users.
  • Uses the video.upload and video.publish scopes to upload and publish content.
  • Uses the user.info.basic scope solely to confirm the authenticated account identity.
  • Access tokens are stored securely on our own servers and are never shared with third parties.

Our use of TikTok data is governed by the TikTok Privacy Policy and the TikTok Platform Terms of Service.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect anonymous usage data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. We use Google Analytics to understand site performance; data collected is aggregated and anonymised.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process.
  • With service providers (e.g. hosting, analytics) who process data on our behalf under appropriate data processing agreements.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy. TikTok access tokens are refreshed periodically and revoked when no longer needed.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. Access tokens and API credentials are stored securely and never exposed publicly.

8. Your Rights

If you believe we hold any personal data about you and wish to request access, correction, or deletion, please contact us. We will respond in accordance with applicable data protection law.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of our services following updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via eqc.investments or through our official social media channels.
